Research

Before opening up the posters to the public I looked through them as a collection to identify any sections, or ways that the posters all connected together. Unable to dins any definite and coherant link, except for that of type, layout and style, I played around with the posters on a computer. These screen recordings show how I navigated through a process of organization.
