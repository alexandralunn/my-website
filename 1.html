Freedom One Wheelchair


Freedom One Wheelchair is a new electric wheelchair designed for the foundation Freedom One Life. This innovative wheelchair differs from the mainstream by using a simplified main structure, fewer components and less weight. This results in reduced manufacturing costs.

Freedom One Life is a foundation promoted and created by Alex Papanikolaou to help people with mobility problems, like himself who suffers from cerebral palsy. He contacted me looking for a product designer more than a year ago with an exciting project on his hands.

Alex is trying to create a new model of wheelchair that differs from the current models. The design goals were to reduce weight through having reduced dimensions and using smaller amounts of light materials like aluminium. It is easy to disassemble, simple, appealing with sporty design and powerful motors.

Over several months I have been redesigning and changing the wheelchair to create a final product that fulfills the design requirements.

Taking shape....

The final design is very simple and easy to manufacture, made mainly of aluminium tubes and sheets. The main frame is made of an aluminium tube that supports the main, heavy parts (seat, motors and batteries), and from which the front wheels and feet plate are attached. Inside this frame there is a box to cover the batteries, electronic equipment and motors.

Currently the first prototype of the wheelchair is being manufactured in a factory and will be complete in a few months.
