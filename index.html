Alexandra Lunn (BAHons)

Alexandra Isabella Lunn is a freelance graphic designer who works in a wide range of media that spans from print, to web, to moving image and to sound design. Her main headquarters are in London, but she also has a Glasgow based office. She has worked internationally with artists and design agendies and collectives. Critical thinking is at the heart of her design ethos. She always pays special attention to detail and is always happy to collaborate on any interesting projects.
