Alexandra Lunn (BAHons)

Alexandra Isabella Lunn is a Freelance Graphic Designer who works with a wide range of media and with a wide range of clients. Her main headquarters are in London, but in order to experience her clients background, she is available to set up temporary studio anywhere in the world. With a passion for collaboration, she works internationally with artists, design agengies and collectives. Critical thinking is at the heart of her design ethos. She is always on the look out for working on projects that have a substance and she always pays special attention to detail.
